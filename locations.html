Our Denver Locations

Explore the best parks in Denver for your pet photography session with Picture This Pet.

Cheesman Park

Directions: Located at 1599 E 8th Ave, Denver, CO 80218. Access via 8th Ave or 13th Ave, with free street parking on surrounding streets like Humboldt St and Race St (check signs for restrictions). Head toward the Cheesman Memorial Pavilion or the central meadow for the best pet photo spots.

What It Offers: This 80-acre park features sprawling grassy lawns, groves of trees like American Linden and Colorado Blue Spruce, a stunning neoclassical pavilion, and fountains. Enjoy mountain views and a connection to the Denver Botanic Gardens.

Why It’s Great for Pet Shots: The open meadows give pets space to run and play for candid action shots, while shaded groves offer soft lighting for portraits. The pavilion and fountains add elegant backdrops, and the Botanic Gardens nearby provide floral elements for unique compositions.

City Park

Directions: Located at 2001 Colorado Blvd, Denver, CO 80205, east of downtown. Enter via 17th Ave or York St, with parking along the park’s roads or at lots near the Denver Zoo or Denver Museum of Nature & Science. Ideal photo spots are near Ferril Lake, the flower gardens, or the Martin Luther King Memorial.

What It Offers: A 330-acre park with Ferril and Duck Lakes, flower gardens, fountains, a golf course, and attractions like the Denver Zoo and Denver Museum of Nature & Science. Features rolling lawns, tree-lined paths, and skyline views with the Rocky Mountains in the background.

Why It’s Great for Pet Shots: The lakes and fountains create reflective, scenic backdrops, while the flower gardens add vibrant colors. The open lawns allow for playful pet shots, and the skyline views provide a dynamic urban contrast, blending natural and city elements in one frame.
