Our Locations

We offer pet photography sessions at beautiful Denver parks and custom PopUp locations. Choose from our scenic park settings or bring the session to your pet's favorite hangout spots.

Cheesman Park

Located in the heart of Denver, Cheesman Park offers a stunning backdrop with its historic pavilion, open fields, and tree-lined paths, perfect for capturing your pet's playful moments.

  • Historic pavilion and gardens
  • Wide open fields for play
  • Tree-lined paths for shade
  • Central Denver location

City Park

City Park provides a scenic setting with views of the Denver skyline, lakes, and lush greenery, ideal for a vibrant and dynamic pet photography session.

  • Denver skyline views
  • Lakes and water features
  • Lush greenery and trees
  • Easy access and parking

PopUp Sessions

Looking to snag a shot at your favorite dog bar, local dog park, or other special places where dogs love to hang out? We'll bring the magic to your pet's favorite spots!

  • Your favorite dog-friendly venues
  • Local neighborhood dog parks
  • Pet-friendly breweries & cafes
  • Custom location coordination
