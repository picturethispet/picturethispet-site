Perfect Photo Spots

Cheesman Park's historic elegance and natural beauty create the perfect backdrop for classic pet photography.

Historic Pavilion

The crown jewel of Cheesman Park, this 1907 neoclassical pavilion provides stunning architectural backdrops and elegant columns for timeless portraits.

  • Neoclassical architecture
  • Elegant columns
  • Historic grandeur
  • Weather protection

Formal Gardens

Beautifully maintained flower beds and manicured lawns create a refined setting perfect for sophisticated pet portraits.

  • Seasonal flowers
  • Manicured landscapes
  • Colorful backdrops
  • Garden pathways

Tree-Lined Promenade

Grand old trees create natural tunnels and frames, with dappled sunlight perfect for artistic and romantic pet photography.

  • Natural tree canopy
  • Dappled lighting
  • Walking portraits
  • Romantic atmosphere

Open Meadows

Wide grass areas provide space for active pets to run and play, perfect for capturing natural, joyful moments and action shots.

  • Running space
  • Action photography
  • Natural play
  • Group sessions

  • Historic statues

Planning Your Visit

Everything you need to know for a successful photo session at Cheesman Park.

🅿️ Parking

Free street parking available on Franklin Street, 8th Avenue, and surrounding Capitol Hill streets. Some metered areas during weekdays.

  • Free street parking
  • Multiple nearby streets
  • Some metered zones
  • Residential area respect

⏰ Best Times

Early morning (6-8am) and late afternoon (4-6pm) offer the best lighting and fewer crowds, especially on weekends.

  • Golden hour lighting
  • Quieter mornings
  • Cooler temperatures
  • Better pet focus

🌿 Seasonal Tips

Spring brings beautiful flowering trees, summer offers lush greenery, fall showcases golden leaves, and winter creates elegant snow scenes.

  • Spring: Blooming trees
  • Summer: Full foliage
  • Fall: Autumn colors
  • Winter: Snow scenes

📋 What to Bring

Bring your pet's favorite treats, a water bowl, and any special toys or accessories you'd like to include in photos.

  • High-value treats
  • Portable water bowl
  • Favorite toys
  • Cleanup supplies
