📝 On The Spot Photography Service Terms

Service Overview

On The Spot photography sessions are informal, on-location photo sessions that may take place at various venues including but not limited to: veterinary offices, dog parks, dog bars, boarding facilities, pet stores, or other pet-friendly locations.

Session Details

  • Sessions are typically 10-20 minutes in duration
  • Location and timing may vary based on venue availability
  • Sessions are first-come, first-served unless pre-booked
  • Weather and venue conditions may affect session availability

Photo Delivery

You will receive 3-5 professionally edited digital photos within 2-3 weeks of your session date. Photos will be delivered via email or private online gallery with download access.
